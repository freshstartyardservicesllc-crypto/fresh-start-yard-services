Size Tiers (How We Classify Your Property)

Size Tier Typical Fit What changes as size increases
X-Small
Tiny front/back or small townhouse
 Quick service, minimal obstacles Shortest time, lightest debris, lowest fuel/haul
Small
Standard small yard
 Simple mowing + detail Moderate time, moderate trimming/edges
Medium
Most suburban homes
 More edging + multiple zones More trimming, longer mow time, more bags
Medium-Large
Corner lots / heavier trim
 Additional perimeter and edging More labor, possible multi-visit for cleanup
Large
Big yards / light acreage feel
 Longer mowing + more detail areas More crew time, more hauling, bigger equipment time
Extra-Large
Acreage-style lawns or heavy zones
 Multi-zone work Highest labor/fuel/haul, may require split scheduling
We confirm size during estimate. If your property is between sizes, we price to the next tier up.

Quality Levels (What “Regular / Premium / Ultra” Means)

Level Best For What You Get What’s Different
Regular Budget, maintenance, simple jobs Core service + basic cleanup Focus on function and speed
Premium Curb appeal, cleaner edges More detail + cleaner finish Extra attention to borders, blow-off, and presentation
Ultra “Show-ready” look Highest detail, deeper cleanup Most time spent on precision + final polish
Quality level affects labor time. Ultra is for “I want it perfect” jobs.
