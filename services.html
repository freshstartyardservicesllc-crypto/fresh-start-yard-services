What Affects Pricing

Your final quote is based on real job conditions. These are the biggest drivers:

  • • Property size tier (X-Small → XL)
  • • Overgrowth level + time required
  • • Access: gates, slopes, obstacles, parking distance
  • • Debris volume + disposal/haul-away needs
  • • Quality tier (Regular / Premium / Ultra)
  • • Materials (mulch/rock/plants/seed/epoxy) + delivery
Want an exact quote fast? Submit the contract or quote request with photos.

Lawn Mowing (Residential + Small Commercial)

A clean, consistent cut with a professional finish. Best results come from weekly or bi-weekly scheduling during peak season.

What’s Included

  • • Mowing all accessible turf areas in scope
  • • Blow off hard surfaces (as needed)
  • • Visual cleanup of work area

Typical Time Range

X-Small to Medium: 20–60 min

Large to XL: 60–120+ min

Time varies by trim/edges, obstacles, and overgrowth.
For starting minimums by size tier, see Pricing → Mowing.

Trimming & Edging

This is the “professional” look—crisp edges, detailed line work, and cleaner borders.

What’s Included

  • • Trimming around fences, poles, beds, and borders
  • • Edging along sidewalks/driveways (where applicable)
  • • Blow-off for clean finish

Typical Time Range

Small yards: 20–45 min

Medium–XL: 45–120+ min

Premium/Ultra tiers add time for polish + deeper border cleanup.

Yard Cleanups (Spring/Fall + One-Time)

Full property cleanup: leaves, debris, and general mess removal to get you “reset” and looking clean again.

What’s Included (Typical)

  • • Leaf/debris gathering + bagging (as scoped)
  • • Blow-off hard surfaces
  • • Light bed cleanup (if included)

What Changes the Price

  • • Volume of debris + number of loads
  • • Wet/heavy leaves
  • • Overgrowth level + access
  • • Haul-away and disposal fees

Beds / Weeds / Mulch

This section covers weed removal, bed cleanouts, and mulch installs (labor + finish tiers).

Weed Removal / Bed Cleanout

Pulling/cutting weeds, removing overgrowth, and resetting the bed so it’s ready for mulch or rock.

Price drivers: bed size, root density, overgrowth, and haul-away volume.

Mulch Installation (Labor)

Spreading mulch evenly, shaping clean lines, and finishing for curb appeal.

Materials (mulch + delivery) are separate unless included in your quote.

Pressure Washing

Concrete/driveways, patios, pavers, steps, and moss/algae focus add-ons. Stains vary—expect add-ons for oil/rust.

Starting minimums are listed on Pricing → Pressure Washing.

Gutter Cleaning

Clear gutters and downspout zones to prevent overflow and water damage. Guards/heavy debris can increase time.

Starting minimums are listed on Pricing → Gutter Cleaning.

Landscape Edging Install

Installed edging helps keep mulch/rock in place and gives beds a clean, finished border. Curves and corners increase labor.

Starting minimums are listed on Pricing → Edging Install.

River Rock / Decorative Rock Install

Fabric + rock installs for low-maintenance beds. Costs vary by rock type, thickness, and haul-away of old material.

Starting minimums are listed on Pricing → Rock Install.

Lawn Patch Repair / Overseeding

Fix thin or bare areas and improve turf density. Results depend heavily on timing and watering. We can do the work—client must follow the water plan.

Starting minimums are listed on Pricing → Seeding.

Epoxy Flooring

Garage/basement decorative epoxy systems. Prep is the foundation (clean, grind, repair, moisture checks). Premium systems cost more because failures are expensive.

Starting rates are listed on Pricing → Epoxy.

Commercial / HOA Services

Use the Contract page to request a commercial quote and ask about COI.
