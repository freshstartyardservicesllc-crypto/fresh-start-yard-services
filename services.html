Detailed Descriptions

Use these descriptions on your site to justify pricing and set expectations clearly (reduces disputes).

Lawn Mowing (Residential & Small Commercial)

Standard mowing for a clean, maintained look—great for weekly or bi-weekly schedules.

Typical time: 30–90 min (residential) Frequency: weekly / bi-weekly Add-ons: bagging, trimming, edging
  • Includes: mowing the lawn area in scope + basic blow-off of hard surfaces.
  • Why pricing varies: property size, obstacles, slope, grass height, and trim time.
  • Labor vs materials: labor-only (materials not typically needed).
Tip: For a “finished” look, pair mowing with trimming & edging.

Trimming & Edging

Sharper borders along sidewalks/driveways and detail trimming that makes the property look professionally maintained.

Typical time: 45–120 min High-visibility results Premium/Ultra = extra detail
  • Includes: trimming around edges/obstacles (as scoped) + edging where applicable.
  • Why pricing varies: length of borders, corners, thick growth, and cleanup time.
  • Labor vs materials: labor-only.

Yard Cleanups (Spring/Fall + One-Time)

A full property reset: leaves, sticks, yard debris, and general mess removed so the yard looks clean again.

Typical time: 2–8 hours Most variable service Haul-away: scope-based
  • Includes: raking/blowing, pile-up, bagging, and general cleanup for the agreed scope.
  • Why pricing varies: debris volume, overgrowth, wet leaves, access, and number of loads.
  • Labor vs materials: labor + disposal/bags depending on volume.
Ultra tier is best when you want a “show-ready” yard with deeper detail.

Weed Removal & Bed Cleanouts

Remove weeds and overgrowth in landscape beds so they’re ready for mulch, rock, or planting.

Typical time: 1–5 hours Great before mulch/rock Haul-away optional
  • Includes: pulling/cutting weeds and clearing beds (as scoped) + basic cleanup.
  • Why pricing varies: overgrowth level, roots/vines, bed size, and debris disposal.
  • Labor vs materials: labor-only unless disposal is added.

Mulch Installation

Even mulch spread with clean edges for a refreshed bed appearance and improved moisture/weed control.

Typical time: 2–6 hours Labor + bed prep as scoped Materials vary
  • Includes: spreading mulch evenly + cleanup of loose mulch in work areas.
  • Why pricing varies: bed size, prep needed (weeds/old mulch), and material amount.
  • Labor vs materials: we usually price mulch/materials separately (you only pay what the job requires).

Shrub Trimming & Pruning

Clean shaping for healthier-looking shrubs and better curb appeal. Great for seasonal refreshes.

Typical time: 1–4 hours Depends on count/size Haul-away optional
  • Includes: trimming/pruning for the shrubs in scope + cleanup.
  • Why pricing varies: shrub height/density, hedge length, and debris volume.
  • Labor vs materials: labor-only unless disposal is added.

Leaf Cleanup & Haul-Away

Blow, bag, and remove leaves so the yard stays neat throughout leaf season.

Typical time: 2–6 hours Volume-based Wet leaves = more labor
  • Includes: leaf collection and cleanup for the agreed areas.
  • Why pricing varies: volume, wet/heavy leaves, access, and number of loads.
  • Labor vs materials: labor + bags/disposal depending on volume.

Debris Removal (Yard Debris Only)

Remove yard debris piles as a defined scope (sticks, branches, leaves, yard waste).

Typical time: 1–4 hours Load-size based Yard debris only
  • Includes: loading and disposal of yard debris in scope.
  • Why pricing varies: pile size/weight, access, and disposal requirements.
  • Labor vs materials: labor + disposal.

Small Landscape Installs

Simple upgrades like bed refreshes, plants, and small layout improvements for curb appeal.

Typical time: 3–10 hours Scope-based Materials vary
  • Includes: install labor + layout (as scoped).
  • Why pricing varies: bed size, prep needs, and number/type of materials.
  • Labor vs materials: materials usually separate unless included in your quote.

Gutter Cleaning

Clear gutters and downspouts to prevent overflow, roofline staining, and drainage problems.

Typical time: 45–150 min 1-story vs 2-story Downspout flush add-on
  • Includes: debris removed from gutters + basic cleanup in work areas.
  • Why pricing varies: number of stories, roof pitch, gutter guards, debris volume, and access.
  • Labor vs materials: labor-only (repairs quoted separately if requested).

Pressure Washing (Concrete / Pavers)

Deep clean for driveways, walkways, patios, and pavers. Great for removing algae, dirt, and surface buildup.

Typical time: 1–4 hours Sq ft drives price Stain treatments add-on
  • Includes: surface wash + rinse + basic cleanup.
  • Why pricing varies: square footage, algae/moss level, stains (oil/rust), water access, and surface type.
  • Labor vs materials: labor + equipment; chemicals/spot treatments added only if needed.

Landscape Edging Install (Plastic/Metal)

Install edging to create clean bed lines and keep mulch or rock contained. Strong curb appeal upgrade.

Typical time: 2–6 hours Priced by linear feet Curves/corners increase labor
  • Includes: bed line layout + edging install for the scoped areas.
  • Why pricing varies: linear footage, hard soil, roots, and number of curves/corners.
  • Labor vs materials: edging material can be included or priced separately (we confirm in writing).
Best practice: bundle edging with bed redefine and mulch/rock for the best “before/after.”

River Rock / Decorative Rock Install

Fabric + rock install for long-lasting, low-maintenance beds and clean lines.

Typical time: 4–12 hours Sq ft-based Haul-away add-on
  • Includes: prep as scoped + fabric + spreading rock evenly.
  • Why pricing varies: square footage, depth, border complexity, access, and haul-away needs.
  • Labor vs materials: rock cost varies a lot by type and delivery—materials are quoted clearly.

Lawn Patch Repair / Overseeding

Repair thin spots and improve turf density. Best results depend on watering and seasonal timing.

Typical time: 45–180 min Watering required for results Prep level varies
  • Includes: surface prep + seed + topdress (as scoped).
  • Why pricing varies: patch size, soil condition, topsoil needs, and prep effort.
  • Labor vs materials: seed/topsoil/straw may be included or itemized depending on the quote.
We’ll confirm expectations: if watering isn’t possible, results may be limited.

Epoxy Flooring (Garage / Basement / Decorative)

Professional epoxy coating systems where prep determines longevity and finish quality.

Typical time: 1–3 days Prep is the job Cure time matters
  • Includes: prep + coating system as quoted (solid/flake/metallic). We confirm cure windows and usage rules.
  • Why pricing varies: grinding/prep needs, cracks, moisture issues, design complexity, and topcoat system.
  • Labor vs materials: both are major—systems are priced per sq ft to match your finish selection.
Luxury designs (3D river floors) are custom-quoted due to complexity and materials.
