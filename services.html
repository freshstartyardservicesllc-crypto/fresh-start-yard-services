What Affects Pricing

Your final quote is based on real job conditions. These are the biggest drivers:

  • • Property size tier (X-Small → XL)
  • • Overgrowth level + time required
  • • Access: gates, slopes, obstacles, parking distance
  • • Debris volume + disposal/haul-away needs
  • • Quality tier (Regular / Premium / Ultra)
  • • Materials (mulch/rock/plants/seed/epoxy) + delivery

Lawn Mowing (Residential + Small Commercial)

A clean, consistent cut with a professional finish. Best results come from weekly or bi-weekly scheduling during peak season.

What’s Included

  • • Mowing all accessible turf areas in scope
  • • Blow off hard surfaces (as needed)
  • • Visual cleanup of work area

Typical Time Range

X-Small to Medium: 20–60 min

Large to XL: 60–120+ min

Time varies by trim/edges, obstacles, and overgrowth.

Beds / Weeds / Mulch

This section covers weed removal, bed cleanouts, and mulch installs (labor + finish tiers).

Weed Removal / Bed Cleanout

Pulling/cutting weeds, removing overgrowth, and resetting the bed so it’s ready for mulch or rock.

Price drivers: bed size, root density, overgrowth, and haul-away volume.

Mulch Installation (Labor)

Spreading mulch evenly, shaping clean lines, and finishing for curb appeal.

Materials (mulch + delivery) are separate unless included in your quote.
